Golf Courses

The number one golf destination in the Caribbean and Latin America, Dominican Republic, delights its visitors with its twenty-five golf courses of renowned designers, surrounded by stunning coastline, with majestic mountains in the background, and “fairways” of lush greenery.

Here golfers enjoy well-manicured fields, so they can relax in a golf cart and listen to the expert advice of the caddies on how to play each hole as they move on to the next. There are six fields on the coasts and 20 in operation in the interior, including a field on the cliff of a river and another in the mountains.
